# GymKioskApp - New Features Quick Start Guide

## 🎯 What's New

Your GymKioskApp now includes three powerful engagement features:
1. **Workout Analytics** - Track your fitness progress
2. **Difficulty Levels** - Choose exercises at your skill level  
3. **Gamification** - Earn badges and maintain streaks

---

## 📊 Workout Analytics

### Access Analytics
1. From the main screen, click **"📊 My Analytics"** button
2. View your workout statistics and history

### What You'll See
- **Total Workouts**: How many you've completed
- **This Month**: Workouts completed this month
- **Avg Duration**: Average workout length (minutes)
- **Current Streak**: Consecutive days exercising

### Favorite Muscle Groups
Shows the muscle groups you exercise most frequently with counts

### Recent Workouts
Timeline of your last 10 workouts showing:
- Date and time
- Muscle groups targeted
- Number of exercises
- Duration

---

## 💪 Difficulty Levels

### How It Works
When selecting exercises for a muscle group, you'll see difficulty filter buttons:
- **🟢 Beginner** - Start here if new to the exercise
- **🟡 Intermediate** - For experienced users
- **🔴 Advanced** - For seasoned athletes

### Using Difficulty Filter
1. Click a muscle group (e.g., "Chest")
2. Click difficulty button to filter exercises
3. "All" shows all exercises
4. Filter persists during your selection

### Exercise Difficulty Indicators
Each exercise shows a colored emoji:
- 🟢 = Beginner level
- 🟡 = Intermediate level
- 🔴 = Advanced level

## 🏆 Gamification System

### Earn Badges
Complete specific goals to earn 10 different badges:

**Workout Count Badges:**
- 🎯 **First Step** - Complete 1 workout
- 💪 **Getting Started** - Complete 10 workouts
- 🔥 **Fitness Enthusiast** - Complete 50 workouts
- 👑 **Legendary** - Complete 100 workouts

**Streak Badges:**
- 🏆 **Week Warrior** - 7-day consecutive streak
- ⭐ **Month Master** - 30-day consecutive streak

**Specialty Badges:**
- Back specialist, leg day legend, quick finisher, marathon warrior

### Track Your Streak
Your current workout streak (consecutive days) appears at the top of analytics.
- Win by completing at least 1 workout per day
- Streak resets if you miss a day

### Badges in Progress
See your next 5 badges and progress toward earning them:
- Progress bar shows completion percentage
- "0/10" means you've done 0 of 10 required things

### View Your Achievements
In **"📊 My Analytics"** → **"🏆 Earned Badges"** section
- Green badges = earned
- Gray badges = not yet earned

## 🚀 Getting Started

### First Time
1. Create a user profile (required for analytics)
2. Enter your PIN (4 digits)
3. Start working out!

### Building Your Profile
1. Select muscle group → Choose difficulty → Pick exercises
2. After workout, your stats automatically update
3. Streaks calculated based on workout dates
4. Badges awarded when goals met

### Tips for Success
- **Earn badges**: Complete different numbers of workouts
- **Build streaks**: Work out every day (or every other day)
- **Progress badges**: Track progress toward your next badge
- **Try difficulties**: Mix beginner, intermediate, advanced

## ⚙️ Technical Details

### Data Storage
- All data stored locally on the kiosk
- No cloud sync (offline-first design)
- Data persists between sessions
- Cleared only when user profile deleted

### Workout Recording
Automatic - happens when you:
- Select exercises and complete builder
- Share a workout QR code
- Mark workout as complete

### Analytics Update
Instant - calculated in real-time from:
- Workout history
- Current date/time
- User profile data

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How are workouts recorded?**
A: Automatically when you complete the workout builder or share a QR code.

**Q: Can I edit past workouts?**
A: Not in current version - future update may add editing.

**Q: What if I miss a day - does my streak reset?**
A: Yes, streaks require consecutive days. Missing 1 day resets to 0.

**Q: Are badges lost if I delete my profile?**
A: Yes - profile deletion removes all associated data.

**Q: Can I see other users' analytics?**
A: No - analytics are private to each user.

**Q: How are difficulty levels chosen?**
A: Manually added to exercise database based on skill requirement.
