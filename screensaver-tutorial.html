Step 1: Create Your User Profile
👤

User Selection Screen

Getting Started with Your Profile:
  • Click on an existing user or select "Create New User"
  • Enter your name (e.g., "Rick", "Sarah")
  • Set a PIN for security
  • Choose an avatar/icon
  • Your profile is saved automatically

💡 Pro Tip: Default user "Rick" (PIN: 1234) is pre-loaded for quick start

Step 2: Generate a Workout Plan
💪

Workout Generator

Create Your Perfect Workout:
  • Select "Workout Builder" from main menu
  • Choose Build Type: Single Workout or Weekly Plan
  • Select Workout Type: 💪 Muscle Workouts OR 🧘 Stretches
  • Pick target: Push/Pull routines, specific muscle groups (Chest, Back, Legs), or Stretch categories
  • Choose goal: Strength, Muscle Growth, Fat Loss, Flexibility
  • Select duration: 30min, 45min, 60min, 90min, or 120min

📊 Result: Customized workout with proper format - reps for muscles, time holds for stretches

Step 3: Add Meal Plans & Nutrition
🍽️

Nutrition Planner

Optimize Your Nutrition:
  • Access "Nutrition" from main menu
  • View meal recommendations for each day
  • Meals sync with your workout calendar
  • Pre-workout, post-workout suggestions
  • Track macros: Protein, Carbs, Fats

🎯 Goal: Complete nutrition + fitness = optimal results

Step 4: Share Workouts to Your Phone
📱

QR Code Sharing

Mobile Access in 3 Easy Steps:
  • Click "Share" on any workout
  • Point your phone camera at the QR code
  • Tap the notification to open on your phone

📲 On Your Phone You Can:

  • Track exercise completion in real-time
  • See progress bar as you work
  • Record weight & reps
  • Share with friends
